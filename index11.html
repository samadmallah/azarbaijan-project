About the Retreat

The International Entrepreneurs' Retreat is a premier event designed to bring together entrepreneurs, innovators, and business leaders from around the world. Hosted by The Institute of Management & Entrepreneurship (TIME), this 6-day, 5-night retreat in Baku, Azerbaijan, offers a unique blend of business networking, skill-building workshops, and cultural exploration.

Participants will gain invaluable insights, forge global connections, and experience the rich culture and natural beauty of Azerbaijan, including a day trip to the stunning Shahdag Mountain Resort.

Objectives

  1. Foster global networking among entrepreneurs and industry leaders.
  2. Provide mentorship and learning opportunities through workshops and panel discussions.
  1. Showcase Azerbaijan's cultural heritage and natural beauty as a destination for business and leisure.
  2. Create a platform for entrepreneurs to exchange ideas, collaborate, and innovate.

Who Can Participate?

  • Entrepreneurs and startup founders
  • Business leaders and executives
  • Investors and venture capitalists
  • Industry professionals and innovators
  • Academicians and researchers in business and technology
  • Students and aspiring entrepreneurs interested in global business trends

Participating Countries

Pakistan, Azerbaijan, Turkey, UAE, Saudi Arabia, UK, USA, Malaysia, Indonesia, Germany, Canada, France, Australia, China, Japan, South Korea, Italy, Spain, Russia, Brazil, South Africa, Mexico, Egypt, Nigeria, Argentina, Bangladesh, Philippines, Georgia, Nepal, Vietnam, Netherlands, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland, Switzerland, and other interested international participants.
